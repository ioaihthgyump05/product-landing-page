Various Music Genres

Divided into 3 main genres - Rock/Metal, Jazz and Classical - we offer a wide variety of subgenres to ensure maximum enjoyment of our lessons and practice sessions.

Fully Educated And Experienced Teachers

You will be taken care of by our well-experienced staff with an adequate musical education background.

Music Theory

Playing an instrument is only one side of the coin. Because we put emphasis on a complete musical education we also offer lessons in musical theory.

Individual Lessons And Classes

Not only are our students looked after individually but also classes with additional band sessions are of big importance to us to encourage grouping and socializing among fellow musicians.

Online Accessibility

Whether you're unable to attend every lesson or in need of additional practice, our students have permanent access to our repertory of numerous video-lessons, available online.
